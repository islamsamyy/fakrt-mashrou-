# Marketing & Growth Roadmap

## Phase 2A: Foundation (Weeks 1-2)
- [ ] Landing page deployed
- [ ] Email templates configured
- [ ] Social media accounts created
- [ ] Initial 100 beta users invited
- [ ] User onboarding survey created

## Phase 2B: Early Growth (Weeks 3-4)
- [ ] Product Hunt launch (optional)
- [ ] Launch week email campaign
- [ ] Referral program goes live
- [ ] First cohort analysis (signup → investment)
- [ ] Collect user feedback

## Phase 2C: Acceleration (Weeks 5-8)
- [ ] Partner with accelerators
- [ ] Content marketing (blog/guides)
- [ ] Paid ads testing (Google, LinkedIn)
- [ ] Community building (Discord/WhatsApp)
- [ ] Monthly metrics review

## Target Metrics (3 Months)
- 1,000 registered users
- 100 active investors
- 50 active founders
- 500 investments created
- 50M+ SAR in funded projects

## Content Strategy
- Blog: How-to guides for investors/founders
- Email: Weekly market updates
- Social: Project highlights, success stories
- Webinars: "Getting started" sessions

## Growth Channels (Priority Order)
1. Organic (word-of-mouth, partnerships)
2. Content (blog, guides, webinars)
3. Email (drip campaigns)
4. Paid ads (targeted, small budget)
5. Influencers (micro-influencers in VC space)
