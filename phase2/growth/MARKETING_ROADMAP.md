## Executive Summary

Comprehensive 12-month marketing and growth strategy to scale IDEA BUSINESS from MVP (124 projects, 47 investors) to market leader with 500+ projects and 300+ investors.

## Phase 2: Growth Acceleration (June-July 2024)

### Content Marketing
- Blog: 2 posts/week
- Topics: Founder tips, investor guides, industry insights
- Distribution: Blog, email, LinkedIn, Medium, Twitter
- Long-form: 2000+ words with SEO

### Social Media Strategy
- LinkedIn: 4 posts/week, 5K followers goal
- Twitter: 2 posts/day, 3K followers goal
- Instagram: 3 posts/week + daily stories, 2K followers goal
- TikTok: 3 videos/week, 1K followers goal
- YouTube: 2 videos/month, 500 subscribers goal

### Partnerships
- Influencer partnerships (organic)
- Strategic partners (incubators, media, universities)
- Ambassador program (5-10 founders + 10 investors)

---

## Phase 3: Market Expansion (Aug-Oct 2024)

### Paid Advertising (500K-1M SAR/quarter)
- Google Ads (40%): Search, display, YouTube
- LinkedIn Ads (30%): Sponsored content, InMail
- Social Ads (30%): Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, retargeting
- Targets: 3:1 ROAS, <200 SAR CAC, 3-5% conversion

### PR & Media
- Press releases (monthly)
- Media targets: Arab News, TechCrunch Arabia, Reuters
- Thought leadership: Op-eds, speaking engagements
- Pitch competitions sponsorship

### Events
- Q2: Founder Meetup (Riyadh, 100-150, 200K SAR)
- Q3: Investor Roundtable (Jeddah, 50-75, 150K SAR)
- Q4: Annual Summit (Riyadh, 300-400, 300K SAR)

---

## Phase 4: Retention & Scaling (Nov 2024 - Apr 2025)

### User Retention
- Founder support: Monthly webinars, 1:1 calls, Slack community
- Investor engagement: Daily recommendations, monthly reports
- Success stories: 1/month featured, podcast series
- Target: <5% monthly churn

### Product Growth
- Tier 1: Milestone tracking, portfolio enhancements, deal docs
- Tier 2: Secondary marketplace, mentorship, syndication
- Tier 3: AI writing assistant, valuation calculator

### Geographic Expansion
- Q3: UAE (20 projects/month)
- Q4: Kuwait/Bahrain (10 projects/month)
- Q1 2025: Egypt (50 projects/month)
